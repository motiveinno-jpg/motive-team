## Core Features

### 1. AI Export Analysis — "Your Export Strategy in 5 Minutes"

Enter a product URL or upload an image. The AI instantly delivers:
- **HS Code** — 10-digit automatic classification
- **FTA Simulation** — 21 FTA agreements, 59 countries
- **Target Market Analysis** — demand, competition, regulations
- **Cost/Margin Simulation** — logistics, tariffs, exchange rates included
- **Custom Export Strategy** report

> A customs broker takes 2 weeks and $3,000.
> Whistle AI takes **5 minutes, starting at $99/month.**

Technology: Claude AI + Vision API + 3-Expert Panel Simulation + UN Comtrade / Korea Customs / World Bank live data

### 2. Auto Documentation — "One Click, Done"

| Document | Description | Traditional Cost |
|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| Proforma Invoice (PI) | Price quotation | $300-500/doc |
| Commercial Invoice (CI) | Commercial invoice | Days via agency |
| Packing List (PL) | Packing details | Manual work |
| Certificate of Origin (CO) | Origin certification | Chamber of Commerce visit |
| Sales Contract (SC) | Sales agreement | Days of legal review |

**5 document types generated instantly.** Inline cost calculator for real-time unit price reflection.

### 3. AI Buyer Matching — "Buyers Come to You"

- AI automatically recommends **optimal buyers** based on product characteristics
- Real-time in-app chat for instant communication
- No more spending $5,000 at trade shows just to collect business cards

### 4. Secure Escrow — "Zero Risk of Non-Payment"

| Stage | Condition | Fund Status |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| 1. Order Accepted | Buyer payment completed | Platform holds |
| 2. Shipment Confirmed | Manufacturer provides shipping proof | 50% released |
| 3. Delivery Confirmed | Buyer confirms receipt | 50% released |

- Stripe-powered global payments
- 14-day no-response = automatic confirmation
- **Early partners: 0% platform fee** (only Stripe processing fee applies)

### 5. Project Management — "Every Export Stage at a Glance"

Quote → Sample → Contract → Production → Inspection → Shipment → Customs → Delivery → Settlement → Complete

**11-stage checklist** + real-time progress tracking + stage-specific inline action buttons
